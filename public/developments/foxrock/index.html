Foxrock

Meticulously Designed 5 Bedroom Detached Family Homes

Set in the heart of Foxrock on the prestigious Torquay Road, Oakwood is proud to present, the Colt Collection, a boutique development of just four A-rated detached homes. Thoughtfully designed across three levels, these five- bedroom residences offer an exceptional standard of modern family living. These A-rated five-bed family homes present a stunning layout of accommodation, with expansive living areas, custom-built kitchens and wardrobes, four bedrooms on the first floor with a master suite to include ensuite and dressing room, and a first floor that will comprise an ensuite bedroom. These will be truly superior homes with innovative external finishes. These new homes will include all the features that make Oakwood developments so successful such as extremely high levels of energy efficiency, fine finishes and excellent craftsmanship.
